Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I install the app?

You can install the app by visiting the App Store and searching for "MegaMeme". Once found, click on the "Get" button to download and install the app.

2. I found a bug, how can I report it?

We appreciate your feedback! If you encounter a bug, please send us an email at support@megameme.com with details about the issue.

3. How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription by going to Apple's subscription management page.

4. How do I update the app?

To update the app, visit the App Store, search for "MegaMeme", and click "Update" if a new version is available.
